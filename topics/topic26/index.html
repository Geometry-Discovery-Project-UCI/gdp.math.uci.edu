• Newton Line, also known as Newton-Gauss Line, is the line that connects the midpoints of the two diagonals in a convex quadrilateral with at most two parallel sides.
  • In the following animation, let $ABCD$ be a convex quadrilateral. Let $ABCDEF$ be the corrsponding complete quadrilateral with the thre diaognals $AC$, $BD$ and $EF$. Then the midpoints of these three diagonals are collinear. The red line is the Newton Line of the convex quadrilateral $ABCD$ or the complete quadrilateral.
